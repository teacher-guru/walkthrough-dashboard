📊 Research-Based Percentage Calculations:

  • • Student Engagement: Engaged students ÷ Total students × 100
  • • Higher Order Questions: Count ÷ 3 expected × 100 (optimal per 5-min)
  • • Prompting & Follow-up: Count ÷ 5 expected × 100 (questioning research)
  • • Checks for Understanding: Count ÷ 4 expected × 100 (Black & Wiliam)
  • • Standards Aligned: Count ÷ 1 expected × 100 (clear connection)
  • • Release to Independence: Count ÷ 2 expected × 100 (Pearson & Gallagher)
  • • Responding to Text: Count ÷ 3 expected × 100 (close reading research)
  • • Attention to LPS: Count ÷ 2 expected × 100 (differentiation practice)
  • • Co-teaching Support: Count ÷ 3 expected × 100 (Cook & Friend model)
